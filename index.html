Movement Control Order in 2020

Since Movement Control Order (MCO) had been used in Malaysia, all roads, supermarkets and parks were empty. This is because government have promise to whom that do not follow MCO, will be punished. It is start in 18 March until 31 March; MCO Phase 1. Then, government had make a decision to extend this order from 1 April to 14 April; MCO Phase 2. Currently, the situation is still the same for Malaysian citizens because MCO will be continue starting from 15 April until 28 April; MCO Phase 3.
